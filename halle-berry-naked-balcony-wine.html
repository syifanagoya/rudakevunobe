Movie star Halle Berry shocks fans as she strips totally naked to drink wine on her balcony

 Posted on September 28, 2024  |  2 minutes  |  230 words  |  Jenniffer Sheldon

HOLLYWOOD star Halle Berry got her fans talking after posing totally naked on her balcony.

The Oscar winner, 56, appeared without clothes in a picture she shared, showing her drinking a glass of wine.

Halle wrote simply: "I do what I wanna do" alongside a kiss emoji.

The snap shows the Monster's Ball and John Wick star standing on a balcony outside her home enjoying a drink.

Luckily for Halle - who has two homes in California, one in Hollywood and one in Malibu - it was a warm, sunny day.

While many were shocked, fans loved her bold snap, with one writing: "Doing it, doing it, and doing it WELL!!"

Countless others branded her their "queen" or responded with fire emojis.

Another asked cheekily on Twitter: "Is the house next door available?"

While most people were overwhelmingly positive, Halle answered one harsh critic - albeit with a very mysterious retort.

They wrote, along with a snap of her post: "Imagine being in your 50s, still posting nudes for attention in menopause when you should be chilling with the grandkids.
